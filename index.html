Monkey D. Luffy

By: Quizeo, Jhon Llyod D.

The Badass Straw hat Luffy

Current Age - 19 years old.

Birthday - May 5

Affiliation - Straw Hat Pirates

Position - Captain

Devil Fruit Ability - Gomu Gomu no Fruit

Luffy is an easygoing captain who spends most of his days sleeping on his ship.

Luffy's stomach is a nearly endless pit, so he gets hungry very often.

Monkey D. Luffy is the adventurous teenager that puts together the Straw Hat Pirates in his quest to become the king of the pirates.

Luffy can be very goal oriented and single-minded. When a challenge comes his way, he sees it through to the end.

Luffy is extremely loyal to his friends. Anyone that shows him kindness will get a big smile from Luffy and he will make you his friend.
